Team members

We only met each other last Tuesday (Sep 29th) but we already have our team up, organized and going happily full speed towards our shared goals. Here's a brief description of each member:

Note: The photos were taken to loosen up the atmosphere. Consequently there is something purposefully questionable with each photo.

Samuel Marisa – the scrum master

Samuel is an Industrial Engineering and Management student backed with teenage years spent optimizing algorithms in C++, programming Java games, and playing with 3D engines.

His work experience includes developing Django based algorithm evaluation services, multi-user Ruby on Rails applications, and being thrice the head assistant of Aalto data structures and algorithms courses.

Last semester he really enjoyed the multi-stakeholder courses Web Software Development (5 cr) and Operations Management Project (5 cr) both of which included major teamwork components. He received the grades 5 and 4 from them respectively.

He values straightforward communication, adherence to agreed practices, and minimalism in programming. He wants effective teamplay and hates unambiguity and disorganization.

To learn more about him, take a look at his GitHub IO page.

Samuel Marisa – the scrum master

This guy is an open source, computing and technology enthusiast. Has been diagnosed as such since childhood. After having been exposed to a major dose of CS related phenomena in his youth he subconsequently ended up for treatment in the IEM department of Aalto School of Science. He is still receiving his medication, but the condition has only gotten worse.

We are pretty sure the man will enjoy the course, especially its technical and teamwork challenges.

To learn more about him, take a look at his own page.

Iiro Tähkä – aka. Mr. knowledge

Iiro studies Information and Computer Science at Aalto University. He has acquired a wide array of skills and isn't afraid of anything, be it new technology or team work.

Tuomo Perä – the low level guy

Hi my name is Tuomo, and I'm member of team 11.

I'm python fan and I mostly code with python, c, c++. I'm very interested in embedded systems, hardware, computer architecture and graphics. My most recent projects include arm7tdmi emulator (gba nds) in c++ and python based raycaster.

Juho Pekkinen – the software developer

This passionate team worker studies computer science and industrial engineering. He likes to code C/C++ but also handles Python, Java & VBA. Juho gets along very well with different kinds of people, likes to get work done with the first try and takes all challenges as a possibility to learn something new.

Matias Pitkänen – the developer
