Samuel Marisa – Scrum master

This guy is an open source, computing and technology enthusiast. Has been diagnosed as such since childhood. After having been exposed to a major dose of CS related phenomena in his youth he subconsequently ended up for treatment in the IEM department of Aalto School of Science. He is still receiving his medication, but the condition has only gotten worse.

We are pretty sure the man will enjoy the course, especially its technical and teamwork challenges.

To learn more about him, take a look at his own page.
